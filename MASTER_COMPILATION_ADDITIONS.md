
### Spiking Neural Networks & Predictive Neuromorphic Edge Computing (Phase 6)
**Theory**: Leaky Integrate-and-Fire (LIF) models used in biological brains to accumulate voltage over time and discharge abruptly (action potential).
**Application in Origin**: Mobile nodes act as neuromorphic synapses. Instead of constantly polling the network (high battery drain), a node integrates topological packet 'pressure'. When pressure hits a threshold, the node 'spikes' and wakes up fully to process the incoming wave, simulating active predictive filtering. (arXiv 2024-2025).
